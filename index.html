Getting Started
  1. Enter your email and a password.
  2. Tap Sign Up to create a new account, or Login if you already have one.
  3. If asked, verify your email, then return here and log in.
  4. Tap Open Budget to load your budget file.
  5. Add accounts first, then start entering transactions, budgets, debts, and recurring items.
Add To Home Screen On iPhone
  1. Open this app in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button in Safari.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add.
  5. Open Christendom Budgeting from the new icon on your Home Screen.

Tab Order

App Guide
  1. Add: enter income, expenses, transfers, and budget-only transactions. Choose No Account when a transaction should affect budgets and reports without changing an account balance.
  2. Dashboard: review monthly income, spending, budget remaining, net worth, account balances, and category spending.
  3. Transactions: filter activity by month, type, account, category, debt, amount, or search text, and import or export CSV files.
  4. Recurring: schedule repeating income or expenses. Due recurring items are created automatically when the budget opens, wakes, or sync checks run.
  5. Accounts: track bank accounts, cash, savings, and credit cards. Credit card balances show red and payments reduce the amount owed.
  6. Budgets: add expected income and expense allocations for the selected month. Reset from defaults copies category default amounts into the month.
  7. Debts: track debt balances, payments, and payoff estimates. Debt payments can be linked to transactions and added to the monthly budget automatically.
  8. Reports: review cash flow, budget performance, categories, income sources, account balances, and debt payoff.
  9. Sync: saves your browser SQLite budget file to your private Supabase Storage file for the logged-in account.
